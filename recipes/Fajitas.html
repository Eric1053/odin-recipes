Fajitas

Are you a poor student who is tired of eating the same old frozen pizza? Then get Fajitas.

Ingredients

  • 1 Chicken Breast
  • 2 Bell Peppers
  • 1 Onion
  • Premade Fajita Spice
  • 5-12 Tortillas

Instructions

  1. Season the chicken, both sides of chicken.
  2. Cook the chicken in oil. Cook both sides 7-8 mins each.
  3. Saute the bell peppers and onion: cut onion and bell peppers into thin slices then saute them in the same skillet.
  4. Mix them together and wrap in tortillas.

Eric's Sandwich

Are you tired of eating simple ham sandwich with the occasional turkey to spice it up? Then look no further than our four animals in one sandwich.

Ingredients

    2 Slices of Wheat Bread 1 Slice of American cheese 1 Thin slice of Ham 2 Thin slices of Turkey 2 Slices of Bacon 1 Chicken Egg 2 oz of Tuna Salt Pepper 2 Lettuce Cups 1 Slice of Tomato
  • 1 oz of Mayo

Instructions

  1. Cook bacon on pan.
  2. Cook egg on same pan over easy style, add pepper and salt to enhance flavor.
  3. Use toaster to toast bread.
  4. Once cooking has been finished prepare slices of bread.
  5. Spread Mayo on toast and to begin putting other ingredients.
  6. In this order ham, turkey,american cheese, bacon, lettuce, tomato, (optional a pinch of pepper and salt), tuna, and egg.
